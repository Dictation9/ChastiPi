Welcome to ChastiPi

To get started, please tell us who you are so we can tailor the experience for you.

As the Wearer: You will be guided to set up the device and then register a keyholder to manage your lock.

As the Keyholder: You will be guided to configure the system and set yourself as the primary keyholder.

Self-Managed: The program will act as your keyholder, releasing your key only after a timer you set has expired.
